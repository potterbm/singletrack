Choosing an interior paint

Interior paint is important to get right because a well-done paint job can greatly improve the look of any room. The most important things to look for in interior paint are washability and durability. Some paints have antimicrobial agents in them to prevent mold and mildew, which is nice if you live in a damper climate or have had trouble with them in the past. Each painting job is unique, so if you have any questions feel free to ask us.

ProMar 200 Zero VOC

  • Contains antimicrobial agents to prevent mold growth
  • Contains no VOCs (harmful chemicals).
  • Touches up well to reduce the need for repaints

Cashmere® Latex Acrylic

  • Nice, silky appearance that looks good from any angle
  • Superior washability and durability
  • Lasts longer than the ProMar 200

Emerald™ Latex Acrylic

  • Our highest quality paint. Lasts longer than the Cashmere® Latex Acrylic.
  • Highest durability and washability
  • Contains antimicrobial agents to prevent mold growth

Choosing an exterior paint

Your needs for exterior paint will change depending on what kind of sun, wind, and weather your house receives. With paint you pretty much get what you pay for, but some of the paints we offer have specific pros and cons. Here are our three options, and as always feel free to ask us any questions you have.

A-100 Exterior Latex

  • Good adhesion, durability, and color retention
  • Efficient coverage, great for routine color changes
  • Backed by a 15-year limited warranty

SuperPaint® Exterior Latex

  • Even better adhesion, durability, and color retention
  • Can be used safely on vinyl siding
  • Lasts longer than the ProMar 200

Duration Exterior Latex

  • Our highest quality exterior paint
  • Backed by a lifetime limited warranty
  • Thicker, more flexible coating for maximum hold and protection
